What are the ingredients in Cümulo?

Every can of Cümulo is made up of hemp-derived Delta-9 THC, CBD, L-theanine, carbonated purified water, natural fruit flavor, and potassium sorbate. We put a lot of love into it too, but that can’t be seen on the label, only felt.

You bet! All of our products undergo rigorous testing during the production process, with each batch being tested by an independent, third-party accredited laboratory. For additional information regarding our testing procedures, please visit our website or contact us at hey@drinkcumulo.com.

What is L-Theanine?

L-Theanine is a naturally occurring amino acid found in green tea, known for promoting calm focus without grogginess. It works by increasing alpha brain waves—those associated with relaxed alertness—and supporting serotonin, dopamine, and GABA levels. Long used in tea and wellness supplements, it’s now a key ingredient in modern functional beverages like Cümulo.
